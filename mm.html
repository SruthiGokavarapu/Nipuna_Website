MODEL MARATHON

DESCRIPTION

For all those who love to illustrate their ideas and thoughts in a visionary way, it is time to add fuel to the fire. Yes!! You are right. Get ready to showcase your amazing skills on the exact platform "XPORIUM - Let Your Creativity Speak". In this modern era, the movies and advertisements are highly digitalized. Somewhere between this evolution of several creations, a great means of presenting posters was pushed to expiration. Nipuna '21 invites you to step towards the exploration and have an enriching experience at this powerful platform to uncover your exceptional capabilities.


RULES

1. Each team may contain a maximum of 2 members
2. All the team members should be present during the event. There will be an interactive session with the judges, other participants and students during the time allotted for the event.
3. The presentation should last a maximum of 8 minutes. There will be a 2 minutes query and answer session following it.
4. The participants should have Zoom app and internet facility.

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA

The presentations will be judged by the judges of the event and their decision will be final in all cases. Judgement criteria is based on ideology, communication and innovative of the participants. TEAM NIPUNA decision will be final.
