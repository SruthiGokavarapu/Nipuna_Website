Presencia

DESCRIPTION

Presentation!!!! An expression to explore the unexplored and to propose a whole phenomenal idea to the world. Here is an amazing opportunity to showcase your concepts on a remarkable and massive platform in the form of a well-integrating and captivating presentation. It strives to scrutinize the curiousness of young minds by giving your incredulous ideas in a definitive direction. Get fused into our event " PRESENCIA - SLIDE YOUR PERCEPTION" and bring all your creative ideas under one roof.


RULES


1. A team should consist of maximum 2 members.
2. Submission of Abstract should be in the form of IEEE format with not more than 300 words.
3. Only participants who qualify in preliminary abstract submission will be eligible for presentation.
4. The presentation should last a maximum of 6 minutes. There will be a questionary session followed by for 2 minutes.
