ABOUT US

A chapter of Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), New Delhi was established on May 1st, 1995 in Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College, Bhimavaram with the main motto of bridging the gap between the industry and the students. ISTE is always known for the quality of the outgoing creators who are enriched with acquaintance and skills. The organization has always backed the students by providing a platform to stabilize themselves as unique individuals. As a part of expanding student's managerial and technical skills, A National Level Techno-Management Symposium, NIPUNA was set into motion for 15 years and till date, this symposium adheres to the standards of the organization. Encompassing a plethora of technical, non-technical and managerial events, NIPUNA strives to UNLEASH THE TALENT WITHIN.



OUR THEME

Every year, NIPUNA embraces a theme that portrays the essence of the fest. This time, it is "CHRONOMANCY - MYSTIC REALM OF SPACE". On June 25 and 26, assemble all your creative and imaginative minds to discover a completely new realm and to mark the beginning of a new civilization in outer space. Participate in NIPUNA '21 to delve deeper into fantasy in a mystic realm that is woven by exhilarating spells of magic in a new dimension of time.

CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM


Campus Ambassador Program is being the face of a firm, organization, company, etc. Through the campus ambassador program, you can know about the events transparently which increases your interest to avail the opportunities available.

Main tasks for Campus Ambassador Program:

  • Social Media Marketing

    Campus Ambassador has to circulate the e-posters through email, mailing-list, WhatsApp, Facebook, and Twitter. They should be up to date about all the programs and events organised by ISTE and NIPUNA'21. Utilize official website and social media to spread awareness amongst students.

  • In college promotion

    College promotion includes displaying all the banners and other information on their social media pages and among their groups to promote ISTE and the events done by ISTE.

  • The campus ambassador plays an important role in building their relationship with other colleges. The campus ambassadors have to help their seniors organize events for NIPUNA’21 online in association with ISTE.

  • Depending on the expertise and background of the ambassadors, they will be divided into different functions, namely, volunteer, photographer, rapporteur, content writer, etc.
Advantages of being a Campus Ambassador:
  • It develops your multiple skills such as leadership, public speaking, etc.
  • Enhance your resume.
  • Build your marketing and communication skills from scratch.
  • Participate in exciting activities.
  • Experience professional growth by solving real-world problems.
  • Campus Ambassador become the face of the college.
  • Represent ISTE from the comfort of your home.
  • Prestigious certificate as a campus ambassador.
  • Promote SRKRISTE and the importance of learning interpersonal skills among your peers.
  • Help your peers learn new skills via events conducted.
  • This position is tenured, part-time, voluntary, and stipend based on performance.
  • Less time taking- just has to work 7 hours a week.
Eligibility:
  • Should be a self-motivated and responsible individual with excellent organizational, managerial and communication skills.
  • Should have a desire to bring positive social change.
  • Undergraduates from any Engineering College can apply.
Terms and Conditions:
  • ISTE is looking for a commitment of at least 1 month.
  • A certificate of appreciation will be awarded on successful completion of duties assigned in the assigned tenure period.
