Struqta

DESCRIPTION

Civil engineers have served the world since the time of ancient civilisations and are continuing to do so till today, creating with the hearts and designing with the brains. They fantasize about building skyscrapers and shaping awe-striking architectures. Do you wish to code and decode every brick that makes up a stunning structure? Then don't put it off any longer! We are bringing you the biggest of opportunities "STRUQTA - CONCEPT TO CREATION" in the most celebrated NIPUNA '21 for you to think out of the box and pave a road of your choice. Let your imagination run! Be the first in the race to bring out the hidden Gustave Eiffel within you.


RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. It is a Civil engineering related technical event in Nipuna'22.
2. All the students related to Civil background stream can participate.
3. Entry fee is 15/- only.
4. Event consists of 3 rounds.
5. Individual participation.
6. Core knowledge will be tested here in different aspects in all the rounds.
7. From the event STRUQTA, you can experience the real civil engineering challenges that you encounter in construction field.
